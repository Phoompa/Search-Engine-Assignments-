the greedy dog once upon time dog managed to steal large steak from butcher shop and ran into the woods to eat it in peace on reaching the banks of stream he happened to see his face reflected in the water never for moment thinking that he was looking at himself in the water what he thought he saw was another dog holding large steak in its mouth being greedy dog he jumped into the stream to snatch the other dog meat of course the reflection vanished and he could see no sign of dog or steak only then did he realize that when he barked to frighten the other he had dropped his stolen meat unluckily for him the current was swift and the steak had been carried away and though the dog hunted all over he could nt find trace of it which meant that instead of having two steaks he was left with nothing
